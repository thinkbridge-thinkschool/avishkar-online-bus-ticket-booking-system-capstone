Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about booking with BusBooking

How do I book a bus ticket?

Search for buses by selecting your origin, destination, and travel date. Choose your preferred bus, pick your seats, fill in passenger details, and complete the payment. Your e-ticket is issued instantly.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept all major payment methods through Razorpay — UPI (PhonePe, Google Pay, Paytm), credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, RuPay), net banking, and popular digital wallets.

Can I cancel or modify my booking?

Cancellation policies depend on the bus operator. View your bookings in the My Bookings section. For cancellations or changes, contact our support team at least 2 hours before departure.

How do I get my ticket after booking?

After successful payment, your e-ticket appears in My Bookings. It shows your seat number, boarding point, and booking reference. Present it to the bus conductor when boarding.

Do I need an account to book tickets?

Yes, you need to sign in with your Microsoft account to book tickets. This ensures your bookings are securely linked to your profile and accessible from any device.
